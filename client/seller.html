How it Works

  1. Enter the USDT amount you want to sell and the buyer's wallet address
  2. Click "Create Escrow" and confirm the transaction in Phantom wallet
  3. Share the escrow address with the buyer
  4. Wait for the buyer to confirm INR payment off-chain
  5. Once payment is confirmed, release the USDT to the buyer
  6. You'll receive INR off-chain, buyer gets USDT on-chain

Platform Fee: 6% of the USDT amount will be collected as platform fee.
