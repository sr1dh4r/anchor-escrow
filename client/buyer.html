How it Works

  1. Get the escrow address from the seller
  2. Make the INR payment to the seller using UPI, bank transfer, or agreed method
  3. Enter the escrow address and confirm the payment
  4. Wait for the seller to release the USDT tokens
  5. You'll receive USDT in your wallet (minus 6% platform fee)

Platform Fee: 6% of the USDT amount will be deducted as platform fee.
